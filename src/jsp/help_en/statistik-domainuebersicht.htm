Statistic - Domain-Overview

Finally, you have the option of displaying all recipients according to their service provider or E-Mail domain. It could be interesting for you to find out how many of your customers have their E-Mail address with BT, GMX or AOL, or whether a lot of recipients belong to a single company like Siemens or BMW. You may want to create a special mailing for that group of recipients.
E-Mail addresses follow a common format: name@domain.de. The domain overview cuts the first part of the address to the @ character. This leaves you with the domain name. The OpenEMM counts how many recipients there are with the same domain and gives totals by domain name.

  • Targetgroup: The Targetgroup drop-down list allows you to restrict recipients to targetgroups. Only recipients who are in the specified targetgroup(s) will be exported into the statistic.
  • Mailing list: This drop-down list allows you to restrict recipients to mailings. Only recipients who subscribed the mailinglist will be exported into the statistic.
