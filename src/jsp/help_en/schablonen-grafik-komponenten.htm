Templates - picture components

After clicking Save, new tabs will appear: Content, Picture components, attachments, trackable links and send mailing. Here you can make additional entries in your template, if you want to. The tab displays a list of all images and graphics inserted into the mailing using the <img> tag. The left-hand side shows you the URL for the image under External picture component. The right-hand side gives you a preview of your image.:

  • New component: defines the directory to the picture on your harddisk. Everything is correct if you can see your image there. If not, you should check the URL again.
  • Overview list: Using the drop-down list, you can select whether or not to use this element in your mailing. In most cases, Use component is the right setting. By clicking on the Update button, your tell the OpenEMM that you have since changed the file on your server. Note, by clicking on the delete-button, the system deletes the component without any query.
