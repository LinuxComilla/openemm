Statistic - Recipient statistic details

The OpenEMM innovative statistical functions give you a detailed overview, allowing you to check the current status of individual recipients (or a group of recipients) quickly and easily. This feature has the following background: the Internet is a fast medium which changes daily. The same is true of E-Mail addresses. There are frequent changes to an E-Mail address database and in particular immediately after a mailing. It is important to note down such changes. The detailed statistics record subscriptions and opt-outs daily and also points out bounces, i.e. undeliverable E-Mails. This not only allows you to evaluate the success of a mailing, but also helps you to monitor your address database. Moreover, the checking time frame can be adapted to personal requirements. In order to check for changes, select a date from your Detailed analysis and click on one of the numbers.

  • Mailinglist: only recipient statistics from recipients in the specified mailinglist will be shown.
  • Targetgroup: By choosing the targetgroup you decide for which group of recipients you want to get the statistic. You can even compare up to 5 target groups according to their statistic by adding several targetgroup with the add-button.
You see the statistic:
  • Recipient-status: shows the number of unsubscribed users, not valid or temporarily not available E-Mail addresses (Bounces) and active recipients.
  • Empfänger-Mailtyp: Dieser Bereich schlüsselt die ausgewählten Empfänger nach dem Mail-Format auf, das in deren Profil eingestellt ist. Die verschiedenen Mail-Formate haben folgende Bedeutung:
Recipient Mailtype:
  • Text: format without format instructions (bold, hyperlinks, fontsize ...) are not included.
  • HTML: in this format, images and specific formatting may be added to the mailing. By using images from external AGNITAS-servers, your client may get problems with some browser interfaces using for example a GMX-mail account.
  • Offline-HTML: format, images are actually integrated into the E-Mail as a attachment, while in the normal HTML version images are downloaded from the server once the E-Mail is opened.

Detail analyse: Here you can see all analytics for one month, listed per day. If you want to know more about a singular value, click on it. OpenEMM will display more statistics about this value. OpenEMM allows you to see which recipient has (un)subscribed within the last days. With the left and right arrows you are able to see the last and next months statistics out of the archive.

You may export this statistic by clicking on the export-button. OpenEMM allows you to save the analytics as a csv-file on your harddisk.
