Campaign - New campaign

To create a new campaign, go to the Campaigns section in the navigation bar.

  • Open the Campaigns section and click on the sub-menu New campaign.
  • The entry dialog calls up the Name field where you can enter a short campaign description, e.g. Newsletter. The Description should consist of one or two sentences characterising your new campaign. This will make mailing management easier once you have many campaigns which are used by several staff.
  • Click on Save to conclude your entries and close the field.

The OpenEMM will now display the overview listing all existing campaigns, including the one you just created.
