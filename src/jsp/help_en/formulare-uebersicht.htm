Forms - Overview

From the customer's viewpoint, customer-oriented marketing is characterised by courteous treatment. This starts with the subscription to the newsletter. OpenEMM offers you a customised service that responds immediately to the opting in or opting out instructions of customers and prospects, and allows them to change their profile data.

Forms offer a very flexible customer interface in OpenEMM. Customers may register by themselves, subscribe to a mailing list, or change their personal profile data - like for instance the required mail format - directly on the website. A form definition always consists of a starting action, a success form, an error form and a concluding action.
To see details of a form, click on the edit-button. To delete a specified form, click on the delete-button.

  • Name: defines the forms name. This name appears later on in OpenEMM, where you can choose a form.
  • Description: Gives you the opportunity to add descriptive comments to the form.
